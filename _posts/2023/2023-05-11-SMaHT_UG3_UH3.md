The Boyle Lab joins the NIH Common Fund [Somatic Mosaicism across Human Tissues (SMaHT) Network](https://smaht.org/awardees/) through a UG3/UH3 award with Drs. Ryan Mills and Michael McConnell. This multi-PI project will develop long-read and computational methods to identify somatic mosaicism in normal human tissues.
