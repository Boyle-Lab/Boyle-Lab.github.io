Dr. Boyle receives a [NIGMS R01](https://reporter.nih.gov/project-details/10340478) to study mobile element derived chromatin looping variability in human populations. This award supports work to understand how polymorphic mobile element insertions, including LTR13 elements, affect 3D chromatin conformation and gene regulation across human genomes.
